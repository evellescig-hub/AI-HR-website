Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is transforming Human Resources by improving hiring, reducing costs, and enabling data-driven decisions. Natural Language Processing (NLP) allows systems to understand resumes, employee feedback, and communication.

How NLP Works

NLP analyzes text by breaking it into patterns and meaning. It identifies skills, experience, and sentiment from resumes and employee feedback.

Why Companies Use It

  • Faster hiring processes
  • Reduced human error
  • Improved decision-making
  • Better employee insights

Key Applications

Resume Screening

AI reduces screening time by up to 50%.

Sentiment Analysis

Detects employee emotions and workplace issues.

Predictive Analytics

Forecasts employee success and retention.

Real-World Examples

  • HireVue – AI video interviews
  • Knockri – Bias reduction (Canada)
  • Unilever – Automated hiring
  • LinkedIn – Job matching algorithms
